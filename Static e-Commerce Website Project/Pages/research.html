Feedback Form

MAIN HEADER


Similarities

  • The height and width of our main header is similar to that of Discord.
  • Our website has a white logo and white font for its name, which is the case with Discord too.
  • Discord's header and our header have similar positioning of our website name, logo, navigation bar, and feedback form/login link.

Differences

  • Discord uses a white and blue color theme, while we use black and white.
  • Discord has an underlining hover effect on links, but we do not.

SLIDESHOW


Similarities

  • Our website has the slideshow of images in the home page, similar to Daraz.
  • The slideshows of both websites scroll automatically.

Differences

  • Daraz's slideshow images have square borders, while ours are rounded.
  • There are dots to indicate which image the slideshow is currently on in Daraz, while our slideshow does not.
  • Daraz's slideshow has a navigation sidebar to its left, while our slideshow does not have anything to its sides.

TOP PRODUCTS


Similarities

  • We have a similar way of presenting our top products to that of Adorama.
  • Both Adorama as well as our website have a scrolling feature in our Top Products section.

Differences

  • We use a black and white theme, while Adorama uses white and dark blue theme.
  • Adorama has arrows for scrolling, but we do not.

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION


Similarities

  • Our way of presenting the product in the box is similar to that of Speedcubeshop, i.e. product image, star rating, product name, price, and add to cart buttons have similar layout in both Speedcubeshop as well as our website.

Differences

  • We use a black and white color theme, while Speedcubeshop uses a primarily white theme.
  • We have rounded borders instead of square borders like Speedcubeshop does.
  • We have also presented the original price along with the discounted price.
  • Our add to cart buttons appear at the bottom of every product box, rather than immediately after the content above them like in Speedcubeshop.
  • We do not have hover effects on product names.

FOOTER


Similarities

  • Both Adobe and our website have horizontally displayed social media icons.
  • Both websites have copyright details in their footer.

Differences

  • Adobe uses a black and grey color theme, while we use black and white.
  • The icons used in our website are different than that used in Adobe.
  • Our footer has no elements other than the social media links and copyright details, while Adobe has a lot more.
